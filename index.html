Maria Hughes

Soprano

About

Wexford native Maria Hughes has recently completed training at the Northern Ireland Opera Studio, covering the role of Fiordiligi in Mozart’s Cosi fan Tutte and performing in a series of outreach performances which toured Northern Ireland. In 2018 Maria received the award of Emerging Artist at the Wexford Festival Opera.

Previously, Maria has performed the roles of: Miss Copeland Dinner at Eight (Wexford Festival Opera), Molly Ulysses (Trilling Trilling Productions), Agrippina Agrippina (Royal Conservatoire of Scotland), Miss Wingrave Owen Wingave (RCS), The Mother Hansel and Gretel (St Magnus Festival), Marie Koanga (Wexford Festival Opera), The Forrester’s Wife The Cunning Little Vixen (British Youth Opera), Laetitia the Old Maid and the Thief (Opera Castalia), Mercedes Carmen (Opera Theatre Company), and Miss Jessel Turn of the Screw (DIT Conservatory of Music and Drama). Maria completed her studies at Alexander Gibson Opera School, Royal Conservatoire of Scotland in 2017. She is an alumnus of the young associated artist program with Opera Theatre Company. Maria has previously been part of chorus’ with Wexford Festival Opera, The Royal Conservatoire of Scotland, Wide Open Opera, Lyric Opera, Opera Ireland and Glasthule Opera.
